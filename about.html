About Me

Hi, I’m Heiko, a freelance Graphic Designer with over 5 years of hands-on experience in UX, Web, and SEO.

I focus on the human element in every design process, and I combine creativity with data-driven strategies. My expertise ranges from crafting user-friendly web and mobile interfaces to implementing gamification techniques for engagement. For me, user research and workshop facilitation play pivotal roles. I’m also well-versed in design for accessibility, ensuring digital spaces are inclusive for everyone.
Working within multidisciplinary teams has honed my communication skills and resourcefulness. Whether it’s managing budgets or advocating for design ideas, I bring clarity, collaboration, and efficiency to the table.
Let’s create designs that not only look great but are also rooted in purpose and usability.
